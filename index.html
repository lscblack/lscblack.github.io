ABOUT OUR MISSION

We decided to tackle the grand challenge of education in Rwanda.

Mission Statement

Our think tank mission is to improve the standard of education in Rwanda through advocacy and training of teachers in order to have well-trained teachers that can identify and address the unique needs of their students.

Problem Statement

In Rwandan public primary schools teachers are often poorly trained and sometimes even not trained at all, with UNICEF reporting that about 20% of primary and secondary teachers don’t even have the basic requirements. This can lead to students feeling disengaged from the learning process and having lower grades and self-esteem.

Solution

The Education Empowerment Initiative aims to address Rwanda's lack of teachers and enhance teacher training through practical and affordable workshops. In collaboration with the government and education authorities, we offer accessible workshops with online options supported by scholarships and subsidies. We emphasize continuous professional development, follow-up support, and mentoring, encouraging schools to invest in their teachers' growth.
